    • About Aanchal

    Aancha believes that becoming a parent is one of the most challenging and rewarding things you'll ever do. As the world's number one digital parenting resource reaching millions of new and expectant parents each month, our vision is to create a world of healthy pregnancies, thriving children, and confident parents.

    • About the Aanchal Community

    Aanchal's online Community is lively, supportive, and honest. With more than 9 million members worldwide and thousands of birth clubs and other groups, the Community is a welcoming place to connect with other parents and celebrate the journey of pregnancy and parenting. Please keep in mind that everything you read in our Community areas comes from other members. Because our Community is so large, Aanchal isn't able to monitor, review, or verify all information posted by our members. Our professional moderators enforce our Community guidelines and review all content reported to BabyCenter staff. Our Community is governed by our Terms of Use.


    Medical Advisory Board

    Any content containing medical information or guidance on health or development is reviewed by a doctor or healthcare professional on Aanchal's Medical Advisory Board. (For example, an article on the stages of labor would be reviewed by an ob-gyn, and a video on breastfeeding by a lactation consultant.) These experts help make sure BabyCenter provides the most complete and accurate pregnancy and parenting information possible. Our advisory board members are highly respected professionals at the top of their fields who are selected based on their range of knowledge and experience. Their specialties include fertility, obstetrics, midwifery, pediatrics, child development, and emotional health. See our medical advisory board.
